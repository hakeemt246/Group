More About Us

As a mainly web-oriented business, our biggest marketing challenge will be getting potential customers who view our real world advertisements to actually visit our online website. The use of online guerrilla marketing is the main strategy we will implement to overcome this challenge. QR Codes, email blast, social media ads and other such promotions all play a vital role in overcoming such a challenge as they provide direct linkage to the site that is user friendly. This strategy will prove to be efficient in generating online traffic to the website as it is generally inexpensive in comparison to traditional marketing such as newspapers and billboards. Guerrilla marketing will keep our firms in potential customers thoughts often and as our main product is cakes this will prove beneficial as the average household buys as least two cakes each year for special occasions.Dream Cakes will take a cost-based pricing approach which allows us to dictate an easily justifiable price as costs are easy to calculate. In efforts to keep pricing as reasonable as possible, we have also chosen to directly distribute to our customer through delivery.
